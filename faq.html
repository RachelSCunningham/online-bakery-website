Frequently Asked Questions

  • I have decided to place an order. How soon can you deliver to me?
    • Great! If you place an order for baked goods by 4.30pm we will be able to deliver the order to your door by the next morning. Exceptions include speciality cakes, if you wish to discuss options for cakes please Contact Us and we can discuss options.

  • Where do you deliver to?
    • Currently we only deliver within Connaught, although we hope to expand our service in the near future.

  • How much is delivery?
    • Delivery charges are a fixed 5 euro rate.

  • Do you have pick up options?
    • Yes. If you prefer you can pick up the next day for free.

  • Why can't I order smaller confectionary products to be deilvered?
    • We would love to be able to deliver all our products to you, and one day we really hope it will happen! However, as we are still a small bakery it would be expensive for us to deliver our smaller and cheaper products; we just don't have the manpower yet! However, they are still available in store in Sligo every day.

  • Can you do inscriptions on cakes?
    • Yes, we provide and inscription service free of charge.

  • I have a gluten/dairy/egg allergy. Do you have options for me?
    • Certainly! We can make allergy-friendly cakes specific to your needs. Contact Us and let us know what you need.

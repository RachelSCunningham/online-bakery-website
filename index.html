Welcome to Rachel's Bakery!

Established in 2015, Rachel's Bakery is an online Bakery service based in County Sligo.

All our goods are homebaked and made fresh upon receiving orders. Rachel Smith has over 25 years experience as a professional baker. She began Rachel's bakery 10 years ago and took the business online in 2015. Having a passion for baked goods and she wanted desire to ensure that everyone could enjoy delicious quality home made baking delivered right to their door. Currently the delivery services includes our breads and cakes, while there are many other smaller confectionary products available in store. Today there are six full time employees at 'Rachel's Bakery' who all work very hard to provide you with only the very best!
